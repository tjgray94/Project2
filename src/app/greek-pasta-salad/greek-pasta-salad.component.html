Greek Pasta Salad

Favorite this Recipe!

Skill level: Easy

Estimated Cooking Time: 3 Hours 30 Minutes

Estimated Yeild: 8 Servings

Pairs well with:

  • Sandwiches/Paninis
  • BBQ Chicken
  • Hamburgers
  • Grilled Hot Dogs

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups Penne Pasta
  • 1/4 cup Red Wine Vinegar
  • 1 tbsp Lemon Juice
  • 2 cloves Garlic, Crushed
  • 2 tsp Dried Oregano
  • 1/2 tsp Ground Pepper
  • Salt and Pepper to taste
  • 2/3 cup Extra-Virgin Olive Oil
  • 10 Cherry Tomatoes, halved
  • 1 Small Red Onion, chopped
  • 1 Green Bell Pepper, chopped
  • 1 Red Bell Pepper, chopped
  • 1/2 Cucumber, sliced
  • 1/2 cup sliced Black Olives
  • 1/2 cup crumbled Feta Cheese

Directions:

  1. Fill a large pot with lightly salted water and bring to a boil over high heat. Once boiling, stir in the penne and return to a boil. Cook the pasta uncovered, stirring occasionally, until the pasta has cooked through but is still firm to the bite, roughly 11 minutes. Rinse with cold water and drain well in a colander set in the sink.
  2. Whisk together the vinegar, lemon juice, garlic, oregano, salt, pepper, and olive oil, then set aside. Combine pasta, tomatoes, onion, green and red peppers, cucumber, olives, and feta cheese in a large bowl. Pour vinaigrette over the pasta and mix together. Cover and chill for 3 hours before serving.
