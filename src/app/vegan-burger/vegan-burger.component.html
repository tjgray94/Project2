Grillable Vegan Burger

Favorite this Recipe!

Skill level: Intermediate

Estimated Cooking Time: 45 minutes

Estimated Yield: 10 Burgers

Pairs well with:

  • Fries
  • Pasta Salad
  • Potato Salad
  • Corn on the Cob

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups Raw Walnuts
  • 2 tbsp Olive Oil
  • 1 Onion, minced
  • Salt and Pepper to taste
  • 3 cups Rinsed and Drained Canned Balck Beans
  • 2 cups Cooked Rice
  • 3/4 cup Vegan Barbeque Sauce
  • 2/3 cup Cornflake Crumbs
  • 2 tbsp Brown Sugar
  • 2 tbsp Chili Powder
  • 2 tbsp Ground Paprika
  • 2 tbsp Ground Cumin

Directions:

  1. Toast walnuts in a skillet over medium heat, stirring frequently until lightly browned and fragrant, 5 to 7 minutes. Crush into fine pieces using a mortar and pestle or food processor.
  2. Heat olive oil in the same skillet over medium heat. Cook and stir onion until lightly browned, about 5 minutes. Season with salt and pepper.
  3. Mash black beans in a large bowl using a fork. Add walnuts, onion, rice, barbeque sauce, cornflake crumbs, brown sugar, chile powder, paprika, and cumin. Season with salt and pepper; stir until mixture is well combined and holds together. Shape into 10 burgers.
  4. Preheat an outdoor grill for medium heat and lightly oil the grate. Place burgers on aluminum foil and grill until browned, 5 to 8 minutes per side.
