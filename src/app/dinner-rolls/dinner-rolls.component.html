Sweet Dinner Rolls

Favorite this Recipe!

Disclaimer: This recipe requires a bread machine

Skill level: Intermediate

Estimated Cooking Time: 2 hours and 20 minutes

Estimated Yield: 16 Rolls

Pairs well with:

  • Chicken Keema
  • Pasta
  • Meatballs
  • These Dinner Rolls can be enjoyed with just about anything, or even alone!

Ingredients:

  • 1/2 cup Warm Water
  • 1/2 cup Warm Milk
  • 1 Egg
  • 1/3 cup Butter, Softened
  • 1/3 cup White Sugar
  • 1 tsp Salt
  • 3 3/4 cups All-Purpose Flour
  • 1 (0.25 oz) Package Active Dry Yeast
  • 1/4 cup Butter, Softened

Directions:

  1. Place water, milk, egg, 1/3 cup butter, sugar, salt, flour, and yeast in the pan of the bread machine in the manufacturer's recommended order. Select Dough/Knead and First Rise Cycle, then press Start
  2. When the cycle finishes, turn dough out onto a lightly floured surface, and divide the dough in half. Roll each half into a 12 inch circle, spread 1/4 cup softened butter over the entire round.
  3. Cut each circle into 8 wedges. Roll wedges starting at wide end; roll gently but tightly.
  4. Place wedges point-side down on an ungreased cookie sheet. Cover with a clean kitchen towel and put in a warm place, let rise for 1 hour. Meanwhile, preheat oven to 400 degrees F.
  5. Bake in preheated oven for 10-15 minutes, until golden.
