Vegan Sweet Potato Chili

Favorite this Recipe!

Skill level: Easy

Estimated Cooking Time: 1 Hour 25 Minutes

Estimated Yeild: 6 Servings

Pairs well with:

  • Cornbread
  • Breadsticks
  • Quesadillas
  • Rice

Ingredients:

  • 5 tbsp Vegetable Broth
  • 1/4 cup 1/2 cup Chopped Onion
  • 3 cloves Garlic, minced
  • 1/2 Jalapeno Pepper, minced
  • 2 tsp Dried Oregano
  • 2 Sweet Potatoes, diced
  • 2 tbsp Chili Powder
  • 1 tbsp Ground Cumin
  • 1/2 tsp Dried Oregano
  • 1/2 tsp Red Pepper Flakes
  • 1 1/2 tsp Tomato Paste
  • 2 cups Vegetable Broth
  • 1 (28 oz) can Diced Tomatoes
  • 2 cups Drained and Rinsed Kidney Beans

Directions:

  1. Heat 5 tbsp of vegetable broth in a large skillet over medium heat. Cook and stir onion, garlic, and jalapeno pepper into the hot broth until slightly tender, about 5 minutes. Add sweet potatoes, chili powder, cumin, paprika, oregano, and red pepper flakes to the mixture. Pour remaining 2 cups vegetable broth ove the sweet potato mixture.
  2. Bring vegetable broth mixture to a boil, reduce heat to medium, and simmer until sweet potatoes are tender, 20 to 25 minutes. Stir tomatoes and kidney beans into the mixture and simmer over low heat until flavors have blended, about 45 minutes.
