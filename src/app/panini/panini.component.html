Grilled Panini

Favorite this Recipe!

Skill level: Easy

Estimated Cooking Time: 16 Minutes

Estimated Yield: 1 Panini

Pairs well with:

  • Pasta Salad
  • Fruit Salad
  • Sweet Potato Fries

Ingredients:

  • 1 tbsp Extra-Light Olive Oil, or as needed
  • 2 slices Italian Bread
  • 2 tbsp Mayonnaise
  • 2 slices Cheddat Cheese
  • 3 slices Deli Ham, or more to taste
  • 1 slice Firm-Ripe Tomato
  • 1 Thin Slice Onion
  • Cooking Spray

Directions:

  1. Drizzle olive oil on one side of each bread slice. Spread t tbsp of mayonnaise on the other side of each slice. Pile cheese, ham, tomato, and onion on top. Top with the other bread slice, mayo side down.
  2. Heat a grill pan over medium-high heat, and spray lightly with cooking spray. Place sandwich on grill, weighing it down with a heavy pan. Cook until cheese is melted and grill marks appear, 3 to 5 minutes per side.

Additional notes:

  • This is a basic recipe, easily changed up with prosciutto, salami, or turkey. This is also great as a veggie sandwich.
  • French bread can be substituted for Italian bread, if preferred.
  • Spreadable cheese can replace mayonnaise, if preferred.
  • Use of a George Foreman grill is recommended, as it eliminates the need for flipping. If you don't have a grill pan, use a regular cast iron or non-stick skillet.
