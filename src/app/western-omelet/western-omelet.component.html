Western Omelet

Favorite this Recipe!

Skill level: Intermediate

Estimated Cooking Time: 20 Minutes

Estimated Yield: 1 Omelet

Pairs well with:

  • Fresh Fruit
  • Toast
  • Hash Browns (or any Potato)

Ingredients:

  • 2 tsp Butter, Divided
  • 1/3 cup Sliced Fresh Mushrooms (optional)
  • 1/4 cup Chopped Green Bell Pepper
  • 2 Green Onions, sliced on the bias
  • 1/4 cup Cooked ham Strips
  • Salt and Ground Pepper to taste
  • 2 eggs

Directions:

  1. Melt 1 teaspoon of butter in a small skillet over medium heat. Add mushrooms, bell pepper, and green onions. Cook until tender, about 5 minutes. Stir in ham until heated through, about 1 minutes. Season with salt and pepper. Set filling mixture aside in a small bowl and keep warm.
  2. Beat eggs together in a bowl, season with salt and pepper.
  3. Heat the same skillet over medium-high heat. Add remaining 1 tsp butter and heat until foaming. Pour in eggs and cook for 30 seconds. Lift the edges of the omelet so that the uncooked egg runs under the cooked edges and comes into contact with the hot skillet. Shake and tilt the skillet to move the uncooked egg. Repeat until the tpo is set but still moist and soft, about 2 minutes.
  4. Spread the filling over one side fo the omelet. Fold the other half over the filing and slied the omelet onto a plate.
