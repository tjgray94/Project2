Vegan Pancakes

Favorite this Recipe!

Skill level: Easy

Estimated Cooking Time: 15 Minutes

Estimated Yield: 9 Pancakes

Pairs well with:

  • Fresh Berries
  • Maple Syrup
  • Really any breakfast staple!

Ingredients:

  • 1 1/4 cups All-Purpose Flour
  • 2 tbsp White Sugar
  • 2 tsp Baking Powder
  • 1 1/4 cups Water
  • 1 tbsp Oil

Directions:

  1. Sift the flour, sugar, baking powder, and salt into a large bowl. Whisk the oil and water together in a small bowl.
  2. Make a well in the center of the dry ingredients, then pour in the wet. Stir just until blended; the mixture should be lumpy.
  3. Heat a lighlty oiled griddle over medium-high heat. Drop the batter by large spoonfuls onto the griddle, and cook until bubbles form and the edges are dry. Flip and cook until browned on the other side. Repeat with the remaining batter.
